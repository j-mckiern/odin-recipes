Emapnadas

Description

Indulge in the delight of our savory emapnadas, featuring a golden brown crust and a symphony of flavors. These hand-crimped treats are perfect as a snack, appetizer, or a quick meal.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Cheese of your choice
  • Pie Crust Dough
  • 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Cook your Ground Beef
  2. Add taco seasoning to Ground Beef
  3. Let Ground Beef cool
  4. Preheat oven to 400 degrees farenheit
  5. Take Pie Crust Dough and roll it out
  6. Cut into circles (size is your choice)
  7. Place beef and cheese onto dough circles
  8. Close the beef and cheese inside the dough
  9. Use a fork to press the dough together
  10. Coat top of empanadas with egg
  11. Place emapnadas in oven for 15-20 minutes or until golden-brown
